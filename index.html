What We Do

Residential Electrical Services

From EV chargers to full panel upgrades, we handle every residential electrical need with master-level precision.

Fireplace Wiring & Installation

Dedicated circuits, proper grounding, and safe installs for all electric fireplace makes and models.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home charging for Tesla, Ford, Rivian, and all EVs. Fast, code-compliant installs with permit pulled.

Hot Tub & Spa Wiring

Dedicated 240V circuits, GFCI protection, and approved disconnect boxes for all hot tubs and spas.

Air Conditioning Circuits

Properly sized, dedicated circuits for mini-splits, heat pumps, and central HVAC systems. Permitted.

Electrical Repairs & Troubleshooting

Tripping breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights — fast diagnosis and fixes done right the first time.

Panel Upgrades

100A to 200A upgrades to support EV charging, modern loads, and smart home systems. Fully permitted.

Ceiling Fan Installation

New installs, replacements, and fan-rated box upgrades. Proper wiring for any configuration.

Lighting Installation

Recessed cans, under-cabinet, landscape, and smart lighting — clean installs, beautiful results.

Dedicated Circuits

Home offices, workshops, kitchen appliances — dedicated circuits for reliable, code-compliant power.

Generator Hookups

Transfer switches, interlock kits, and whole-home backup connections installed safely and to code.
