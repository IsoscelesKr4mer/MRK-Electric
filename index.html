What We Do

Residential Electrical Services in Issaquah & East King County

From EV charger installation to full panel upgrades and emergency repairs, we handle every residential electrical need across Issaquah, Bellevue, Redmond, and East King County with master-level precision.

Electric Fireplace Installation

Dedicated circuits, proper grounding, and safe electric fireplace installation for all makes and models. Serving Bellevue, Issaquah, and East King County.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home EV charger installation for Tesla, Ford, Rivian, and all EVs. Fast, code-compliant installs with permit pulled. Serving Issaquah, Bellevue, Redmond, and Sammamish.

Hot Tub & Spa Wiring

Dedicated 240V circuits, GFCI protection, and approved disconnect boxes for all hot tubs and spas throughout East King County — Issaquah, Sammamish, Bellevue, and beyond.

Air Conditioning Circuits

Properly sized, dedicated circuits for mini-splits, heat pumps, and central HVAC systems. Permitted.

Electrical Repairs & Emergency Service

Tripping breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights — fast diagnosis and emergency electrical repairs done right the first time. Same-day service available across East King County.

Panel Upgrades

100A to 200A electrical panel upgrades to support EV charging, modern loads, and smart home systems. Fully permitted. Serving Redmond, Kirkland, Issaquah, and East King County.

Ceiling Fan Installation

New installs, replacements, and fan-rated box upgrades. Proper wiring for any configuration.

Lighting Installation

Recessed cans, under-cabinet, landscape, and smart lighting — clean installs, beautiful results.

Dedicated Circuits

Home offices, workshops, kitchen appliances — dedicated circuits for reliable, code-compliant power.

Generator Hookups

Transfer switches, interlock kits, and whole-home backup connections installed safely and to code.
